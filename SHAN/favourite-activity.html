Reading Books

Ink-stained fingers flit across pages, conjuring worlds of wizards, dragons, and whispered magic. My heart beats with the rhythm of epic quests, from battling Dementors at Hogwarts to unraveling Grisha mysteries in Ravka. Tolkien's hobbits and Martin's griffins ignite my imagination, while Leigh Bardugo's crows and Kaz Brekker's cunning steal my breath. For me, fantasy isn't just escapism; it's a vibrant tapestry woven with daring adventures, whispered prophecies, and characters who feel like old friends. So, pass me a goblet of butterbeer, a Lembas bread, or a steaming cup of Kerch broth, and let's delve into the endless possibilities that lie within the pages.
